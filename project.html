Our projects

Crochet Archives

The Crochet Archives contains countless crochet patterns with images for FREE,
which is perfect for both beginners and veterans in crocheting alike!

All of the patterns were contributed by fellow volunteers of Loops and Love.

Yarnbombing

Want to make the community more colourful and lively with crocheting?
Let's share the joy of crocheting with our beloved community with yarnbombing!

Read more about our monthly yarnbombing events here.

Yarn donation

We all have that box of yarns that just sits there while we buy new batches of yarns.
Loops and Love collects and repurpose the yarns as plushies for children.

Read more about how it works and/or sign up to be a volunteer!
