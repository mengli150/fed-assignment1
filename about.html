About us

Hello there, we're Loops and Love!

Loops and Love was founded by four of us during the COVID-19 lockdowns.

Everyone was affected by the pandemic, and some were worse off than the others.

Making use of all the extra time we had at our homes, we decided to create Loops and Love to spread love and kindness through crocheting. We made winter sweaters, blankets and gloves for those who were in need.

Today, Loops and Love offers free crochet patterns contributed by our volunteers and conduct events like yarnbombing, plushy-giving and yarn donations!

All of these efforts were realised thanks to volunteers from all around the globe, contributing to the same goal, to spread love and kindness through crocheting.
